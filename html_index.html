Department Overview

Plant Maintenance Department is responsible for day to day maintenance activities of the Machineries in the Power Cable Plant, Telecable Plant, PVC Plant, Reel Recovery and Rod Mill

Mission

  • Maintain Plant Machineries ate the highest Operating Efficency
  • Identify possible faults and apply Preventive Maintenance
  • Reduce Overall Operating Expenses

Vision

Objectives

Purpose

  • Perform Corrective Maintenance of Plant Machineries
  • Implement Preventive Maintenance for machines and equipments
  • Installation and relocation of machineries

Activities

  1. Breakdown Maintenance
  2. Preventive Maintenance
  3. Condition Monitoring
